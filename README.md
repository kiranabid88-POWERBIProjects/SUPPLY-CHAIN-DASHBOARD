# SUPPLY-CHAIN-DASHBOARD
A 5-page Power BI dashboard analyzing orders, products, suppliers, logistics, and revenue forecasting — covering 3,000+ orders, 200 products, 30 suppliers, and 10 warehouses with $205M in total revenue.

DASHBOARD PAGES

Overview — Total orders (3K), revenue ($67M), profit ($22M), profit margin (32%), on-time delivery (85%). Revenue by month, by category, by warehouse, top 5 products by revenue.
Screenshorts/Overview.png

Products — Total products (200), total revenue ($205M), total/average quantity sold. Revenue by category, product performance matrix (revenue, profit, margin, quantity by category), top 5 products by quantity sold, top 5 brands by revenue.
Products.png

Suppliers — 30 suppliers, average lead time (11 days), supplier revenue ($205.32M), supplier profit ($65.97M). Top 10 suppliers by revenue, supplier profit margin trend, average lead time by supplier.
Suppliers.png

Logistics — Total shipments (3K), on-time delivery (85%), average delivery days (7.99), total shipping cost (856K). Shipping cost trend, shipping cost by carrier, average delivery days by route, transport mode analysis (Air/Ground/Sea).
Logistics Analysis.png

Trend & Forecast — Revenue YTD ($70.82M), revenue growth (52.7%), orders YTD (3K), profit margin (32%). Revenue trend with built-in forecast line, monthly orders/profit trends, revenue by category and by country (map visual).
Trend & Forecasting.png

Each page has its own independent slicers — Year, Month, Category, WarehouseID — and tab-style page navigation buttons

TOOLS

Power BI Desktop · DAX · Power Query (M) · Microsoft Excel · Bing Maps (country-level revenue map),Powerpointc(custom Background)
